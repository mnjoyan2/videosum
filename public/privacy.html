Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What Videosum does

Videosum is a browser extension and web app that summarizes YouTube videos into short highlight clips using AI. All AI processing is performed via the OpenAI API using an API key you supply yourself.

Data we collect

We collect no personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information.
  • We do not track your browsing history or YouTube watch history.
  • We do not store your OpenAI API key on any server. It is kept only in your browser's local storage and sent directly to the Videosum processing server over HTTPS solely to perform the summarization you requested.
  • We do not use cookies or analytics.

Video data

When you summarize a video, the YouTube URL or uploaded video file is sent to the Videosum server to extract audio and generate a transcript and summary. Processed files are stored temporarily on the server and are not shared with any third party other than OpenAI (for transcription and summarization via their API).

Third-party services

Summarization uses the OpenAI API. Your video audio is sent to OpenAI under their privacy policy using the API key you provide. We have no control over OpenAI's data handling.

Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties, except as described above (OpenAI, for the sole purpose of performing the summarization).
